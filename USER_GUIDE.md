## ğŸš¨ Step 5: Report a Hazard

### When you encounter a road hazard:

1. **On the Map Dashboard**, look for the **"Report Hazard"** button
   - Usually located at the bottom-right or top-right of the map

2. **Click "Report Hazard"** - A modal/form will appear

3. **Fill in the hazard details:**
   - **Hazard Type**: Select from dropdown
     - Accident
     - Pothole
     - Road Closure
     - Debris on Road
     - Flooding
     - Construction
     - Other
   
   - **Severity Level**: Choose severity
     - Low
     - Medium
     - High
   
   - **Description**: Briefly describe the hazard
     - Example: "Large pothole on Main Street near intersection"
   
   - **Location**: 
     - Click on the map to select location, OR
     - Enter address manually, OR
     - Use "Current Location" button

   - **Upload Photo** (Optional):
     - Click "Choose File" or "Upload Photo"
     - Select an image from your device
     - Helps other users identify the hazard

4. **Click "Submit" or "Report Hazard"**

5. **Hazard Reported!** 
   - You'll see a confirmation message
   - The hazard marker appears on the map immediately
   - Other users can now see it
